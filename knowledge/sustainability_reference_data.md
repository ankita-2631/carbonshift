# Sustainability Reference Data — Facilities, Fleet & Procurement

Cite this document as "Sustainability Reference Data". Figures are typical UK averages
used for decision support; they are estimates, not a live data feed. CarbonShift never
recommends a change that increases emissions, and locked or single-source items are
always left unchanged.

## Decision thresholds
- A recommendation is rated GREEN when it cuts emissions by at least 15% without raising
  cost more than ~2%, AMBER when it still cuts emissions but trades off cost or impact,
  and RED when no viable improvement exists (locked, single-source, or not technically swappable).

## Facilities (Scope 1 & 2)
- Electricity emissions use the live grid carbon intensity (gCO2/kWh) for the day, so the
  same kWh saved avoids more carbon when the grid is dirty.
- Natural gas heating: ~184 gCO2 per kWh, priced at ~7p per kWh.
- Electricity price is taken from the live pricing model (pence per kWh).
- Typical reducible share by measure:
  - Smart HVAC setpoint + scheduling: ~15–25% of HVAC load.
  - LED retrofit + daylight/occupancy sensors: ~40–50% of lighting load.
  - Raise cold-aisle setpoint + airflow containment: ~15–20% of cooling load.
  - Destratification fans + zoning (gas heating): ~15% of heating load.
- Facilities figures are reported per day and annualised by ×365 for the annual headline.

## Fleet (Scope 1 & 3)
- Tailpipe + lifecycle emissions per vehicle-km: diesel ~200 g, petrol ~180 g, EV ~47 g
  (EV depends on the grid mix when charged).
- Running cost per km: diesel ~£0.22, petrol ~£0.25, EV ~£0.08.
- Electrifying a viable diesel or petrol vehicle typically cuts ~74–76% of both carbon and
  running cost.
- Vehicles flagged as not swappable to EV (e.g. heavy haulage) are left unchanged (RED).
- Fleet figures are computed annually (52 weeks × days driven per week).

## Procurement (Scope 3)
- Each purchase line carries an embodied-carbon figure (kgCO2e) and a cost.
- Lower-carbon alternatives and their cost deltas are supplied per line, for example:
  - 100% recycled paper: ~40% less carbon, similar cost.
  - Refurbished / extended-life laptops: ~30% less carbon, similar or lower cost.
  - Recycled / compostable packaging: ~35% less carbon, similar cost.
- Lines marked locked (single-source, contractual, or compliance-critical) are never
  switched and are reported as RED.
- Procurement figures are already annual.

## Honesty and limits
- Real emissions and costs vary with equipment, usage, route, occupancy, supplier, and the
  electricity mix; treat these as directional estimates for prioritising the biggest wins.
- All recommendations are advisory decision support, not a guarantee.
